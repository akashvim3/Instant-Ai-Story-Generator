Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about AI StoryStream

General Questions

What is AI StoryStream?

AI StoryStream is an advanced AI-powered platform that generates compelling stories, captions, and narratives for live streaming events in real-time. Using state-of-the-art natural language processing, we help content creators, streamers, and journalists create engaging content instantly.

Who can benefit from AI StoryStream?

Our platform is perfect for:

  • Live streamers and content creators
  • Sports commentators and journalists
  • Event organizers and marketers
  • Social media managers
  • Gaming streamers
  • Concert and entertainment venues

How does the AI work?

Our AI uses advanced transformer-based language models trained on vast amounts of text data. When you provide an event description, the AI analyzes the context, understands the tone and style you want, and generates a relevant, engaging narrative that matches your specifications.

Features & Capabilities

What writing styles are available?

We offer multiple writing styles including:

  • Dramatic - High energy and emotional
  • Humorous - Light-hearted and funny
  • Professional - Formal and informative
  • Casual - Friendly and relaxed
  • Inspirational - Motivating and uplifting
  • Suspenseful - Building tension and excitement

How many languages do you support?

We support 50+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Hindi, Arabic, Russian, and many more. The AI generates native-quality content in each language.

What is auto-generation mode?

Auto-generation mode automatically creates stories at regular intervals (every 30 seconds by default) during your live stream. Perfect for continuous coverage of ongoing events without manual intervention.

Can I export generated stories?

Yes! You can export stories in multiple formats including TXT, JSON, and CSV. You can also copy individual stories to clipboard or download them directly.

Pricing & Plans

Is there a free plan?

Yes! We offer a free plan with 10,000 words per month (approximately 20 stories). It's perfect for testing our platform. We also offer a 14-day free trial of our paid plans with no credit card required.

What are your paid plans?

We offer three paid plans:

  • Basic: $9.99/month - 200,000 words, all styles, 10 languages
  • Pro: $29.99/month - 1M words, all features, 50+ languages, API access
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing - Unlimited usage, white-label, dedicated support

Can I change plans anytime?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day free trial, so you can test our service risk-free. After the trial, payments are non-refundable, but you can cancel anytime to avoid future charges.

Technical Questions

Do you have an API?

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include API access. Our RESTful API allows you to integrate AI story generation into your own applications. We provide comprehensive documentation and SDKs for JavaScript, Python, and PHP.

What are the API rate limits?

Rate limits vary by plan:

  • Pro: 1,000 API calls per day, 60 requests per minute
  • Enterprise: Unlimited API calls and custom rate limits

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We perform regular security audits and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

Can I integrate with my streaming platform?

Yes! Our API makes it easy to integrate with popular streaming platforms like YouTube Live, Twitch, Facebook Live, and custom RTMP streams. We also offer webhooks for real-time notifications.

What browsers do you support?

Our platform works on all modern browsers including Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, and Edge 90+. We also have a fully responsive design that works perfectly on mobile devices.

Account Management

How do I create an account?

Simply click "Get Started" or "Sign Up" and provide your email address and password. You can also sign up using your Google or GitHub account for faster registration.

Can I share my account with team members?

Pro plans include 3 team seats, and Enterprise plans offer unlimited seats. Each team member gets their own login credentials while sharing the same subscription and usage limits.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a secure reset link. The link expires after 1 hour for security.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from Account Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data after a 30-day grace period. You can cancel the deletion during this period.
